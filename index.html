Dr.travelProgramme

Your Prescription for a Perfect Getaway

Travel as a form of wellness. We are a travel recommendation company that acts as a personal "travel doctor," diagnosing a client's specific needs and prescribing a curated, wellness-focused trip.

Core Offerings:

- Diagnosis & Prescription: Clients complete a brief questionnaire to identify their travel "ailment" (e.g., burnout, need for adventure, desire for connection). We then generate a personalized travel prescription a detailed itinerary designed to rejuvenate their mind, body, and spirit.

- Comprehensive Guides: Each prescription comes with a full "treatment plan," including packing tips, cultural guides, and wellness activities to ensure a seamless and enriching journey.

Expert Curation: Our recommendations are meticulously vetted by a team of travel and wellness experts, prioritizing quality and genuine benefit over a generic list of destinations.

Key Differentiators:

- Holistic & Personalized: We move beyond traditional travel planning by integrating wellness principles into every step. We don't offer one-size-fits-all trips; we create a bespoke experience tailored to the individual.

- Focus on Transformation: Our goal is for clients to return not just rested, but genuinely transformed and able to carry the benefits of their trip into their daily life.
